Tibes/Tibsi: Traditional Eritrean/Ethiopian Recipe

This is one of our favourites enjoyed by both the Eritrean and Ethiopian members of the group. Tibes is the Amharic name and Tibsi is the Tigrinya name.
It’s a nice easy dish to prepare, and a great food to eat eat before going out drinking with friends. It goes well with Tej – a traditional honey wine.

Ingredients

  1. Onion
  2. 1/2 kilo meat
  3. oil
  4. 1 spoon butter or ghee
  5. green chillis
  6. 1 tomato
  7. rosemary
  8. garlic
  9. salt

Method

First chop meat into small pieces, then put it in a pan for around 3 or 4 minutes. Add onions chopped like a salad.
After 5 minutes add tomato and wait for another 2 or 3 minutes. Add mixed rosemary, garlic and chilli.
Finally, after waiting 3 minutes, add as as much butter/ghee and then salt as you like. Best served with Injira (flatbread).
